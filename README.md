🎯 Project Objective

The goal of this analysis is to:

Understand operational performance of goodcabs across cities

Identify demand trends by month and day

Compare city-wise performance (trips, revenue, distance, ratings)

Measure passenger behaviour (repeat rates, trip frequency)

Provide actionable insights to improve utilization, retention, and revenue

📌 Key Business Takeaways

Jaipur is the top-performing city, offering the highest trip count and average fare.

Surat & Lucknow show excellent repeat passenger behaviour, indicating strong loyalty.

Mysore & Coimbatore present opportunities for growth in new customer acquisition.

Demand is weekday-driven, suggesting strong commuter-based usage.

A 25% repeat rate highlights an opportunity to introduce loyalty or membership programs.
