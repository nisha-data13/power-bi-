CODEX Consumer Insights – Power BI Dashboard

Food & Beverage Industry | Data Analytics Project

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing consumer behavior, preferences, and market trends for the CODEX brand in the Food & Beverage industry. Using survey data from 10,000 respondents across 10 major Indian cities, the dashboard delivers actionable insights to support marketing, product, and business strategy decisions.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate end-to-end data analysis, from understanding raw survey data to creating an interactive Power BI dashboard for stakeholder consumption.

🎯 Objectives

Understand customer demographics (age, gender, city)

Identify consumer preferences related to packaging, ingredients, and pricing

Analyze brand awareness and past usage behavior

Evaluate the effectiveness of marketing channels

Provide data-driven recommendations for business growth

📊 Dashboard Highlights

Total Respondents: 10,000

Total Cities Covered: 10

Average Rating: 3.28

Key Insights

The 19–30 age group represents the largest consumer segment

Compact & portable packaging is the most preferred format

Online advertisements are the most effective marketing channel

Supermarkets are the primary purchase location

Consumers prefer caffeine and vitamin-based ingredients

Top improvement requests include reduced sugar content and natural ingredients

High-response cities: Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Mumbai
