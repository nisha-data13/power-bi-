This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of store sales performance across regions, customer segments, and product categories. It helps monitor sales trends, customer growth, and shipping modes to support data-driven business decisions.

📈 Visuals Included

Sales LY and YoY Growth % by Year – Tracks year-over-year growth in total sales.

Sales by State – Identifies top-performing states and regions.

Sales by Ship Mode – Analyzes performance by shipping class (Standard, First, Second, Same Day).

Sales by Segment – Breaks down sales by customer type (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).

Sales by Category – Highlights revenue contribution from Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture.

🌍 Filters & Interactivity

Regional filters (Central, East, South, West) allow users to explore performance across geographies.

Interactive visuals enable drill-down analysis by segment and category.
